Meet Our Chefs

Chef Luca Marin

Executive Chef

Trained in Naples, Chef Luca specializes in rustic Italian and Mediterranean cuisine. His handmade pastas, wood-fired pizzas, and rich risottos bring authentic coastal flavors to every plate.

Chef Sofia Klein

Pastry Chef

A graduate of Le Cordon Bleu Paris, Chef Sofia crafts delicate pastries and classic French desserts. Her specialties include buttery croissants, crème brûlée, and seasonal fruit tarts.

Chef Harry Reyes

Sous Chef

Chef Harry brings vibrant Latin American flavors to the kitchen with his bold taco creations, juicy chimichurri steaks, and hand-folded empanadas. He's passionate about fusing street-style flair with fine dining.
